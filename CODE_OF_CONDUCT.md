# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss technical facts.
- Avoid harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainer may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in permanent exclusion from project spaces.
